MONKEY TRAIL

Follow the Monkey Trail and meet our playful primates! This trail lets you watch monkeys climbing, playing, and interacting in their natural-like habitat.

Highlights:

  • Capuchin monkeys showing off their tricks
  • Baboon family enjoying the sun
  • Shady paths with vines and jungle sounds

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is located just beyond the Lost Forest, along the eastern path.
